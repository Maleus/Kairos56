## Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 9, 2026

Kairos Prison Ministry — WTSP Chapter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

### Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide:

- **Contact information:** Name, email address, and phone number when you join the Kairos 56 team roster.
- **Communications:** Messages you send to us via email or SMS.
- **Site usage:** Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) are collected by Netlify for security and performance.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party.

### How We Use Your Information

- To send team communications (meeting reminders, schedule updates, prayer requests) via your preferred channel (email or SMS).
- To coordinate Kairos 56 weekend activities and team assignments.
- To respond to inquiries you submit through the site.

### SMS Communications

If you opt in to SMS updates, you will receive text messages from (901) 519-3120 related to Kairos 56 team coordination. Message frequency varies based on team needs (typically 1–3 per week). Message and data rates may apply.

**To opt out of SMS:** Reply STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent to that number.

**For help:** Reply HELP or contact us directly.

### Data Retention

Your contact information is retained for the duration of the Kairos 56 weekend and removed afterward, unless you request otherwise.
